Cape Cod Telescope

After teaching astronomy for the past few years, I realized there is a nearly insurmountable price barrier to students who want to take their own images of the night sky. This website allows users to search the NASA APOD by date for inspiration to request their own astrophotographs to be taken. It also includes an updated astronomical forecast for the location of the observatory and a contact form. Tech used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP

Magic: The Gathering Card Game Player

During the earlier stages of the pandemic, when we were all at home, and my social group was spread across the country, I made this website to allow us to play a game and feel the social connection without being in person. Using the Magic: The Gathering API, this website allows you to keep track of cards in play while virtually playing the card game. Tech used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Plant Identification

After having difficulties identifying plants in my yard, I made this website. Plant.id API allows images to be sent and the plant species in those images to be identified with a confidence rating. Tech used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript

The Dream Blanket Book

The Dream Blanket is a non-profit book written to provide blankets and copies of the book to children living in shelters. This website was made for free in support of a good cause. Tech used: HTML, CSS
